Pasta alla Norma

A typical recipe of traditional Sicilian cuisine and in particular of Catania, which contains all the flavours of summer and is perfect for many occasions.

Ingredients

    • 320g Penne
    • 2 eggplant
    • 700 ml Salsa Souce
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 bunch basil
    • salt
    • olive oil
    • ricotta cheese (garnish)

Steps

  1. We cut the aubergines into cubes with a cutting board and knife.
  2. Let's put them in a bowl inside a colander, add salt and let them rest.
  3. Brown a clove of garlic in oil in a large pan.
  4. Add the tomato puree and basil, cover with the lid and let it simmer to flavour the sauce.
  5. Season the aubergines with a drizzle of oil.
  6. Place them in the air fryer basket and cook at 200Â°C for 10 minutes, turning them every now and then.
  7. Add the cooked aubergines to the sauce in the saucepan and stir.
  8. Cook the pasta, drain it and toss it well in the pan with the sauce.
  9. Once plated, sprinkle with grated salted ricotta.
  10. Pasta alla Norma is ready to be enjoyed, enjoy your meal!
