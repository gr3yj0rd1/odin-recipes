Crostata alle nocciole

A Crostata di Nocciole (Hazelnut Tart) is a traditional Italian dessert made with a rich, buttery pastry crust and a filling that typically consists of hazelnuts, sugar, and sometimes chocolate or cream

Ingredients

for the shortcrust pastry

  • 2 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 100ml seed oil
  • 300g white flour
  • 8g baking powder

for the filling

  • 150g hazelnuts
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 lemons
  • 3 medium eggs

for decoration

  • 50g of hazelnuts

to decorate

  • chopped hazelnuts

Steps

  1. Let's prepare the shortcrust pastry: mix the eggs, sugar and oil with a spoon in a large bowl.
  2. When we obtain a homogeneous mixture, we add the flour and the baking powder, continuing to mix in the bowl until we obtain a workable dough.
  3. Let's move onto the work surface and knead by hand with the help of a handful of flour.
  4. Once the dough is smooth, wrap it in cling film and put it in the fridge for at least half an hour.
  5. For the filling, first beat the egg whites until stiff with an electric whisk.
  6. Add the sugar to the egg whites and continue mixing with the whisk.
  7. Finely chop the toasted hazelnuts using a food processor, then add them to the filling together with the grated lemon peel and blend until smooth.
  8. Once the resting time has elapsed, we take the pastry and roll it out using flour and a rolling pin.
  9. We roll the dough in the rolling pin and place it in a 24 cm diameter tart pan, buttered and floured.
  10. Carefully line the pan with the pastry, then remove the excess edges using the back of a knife.
  11. Pour the filling into the pastry shell and level it with the back of a spoon.
  12. We decorate the surface with chopped hazelnuts and bake in a preheated oven for about 30 minutes, at 170 °C if it is fan-assisted or at 190 °C if it is static.
  13. Let's take our hazelnut tart out of the oven and serve it!
