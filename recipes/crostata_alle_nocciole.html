Crostata alle nocciole

A Crostata di Nocciole (Hazelnut Tart) is a traditional Italian dessert made with a rich, buttery pastry crust and a filling that typically consists of hazelnuts, sugar, and sometimes chocolate or cream

Ingredients

For the base

    3/4 dose of Cocoa Pastry

For the filling

  • 250g hazelnuts
  • 180g sugar
  • 70g butter
  • 3 medium eggs

For decoration

  • 50g of hazelnuts
