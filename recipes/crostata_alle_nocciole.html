Crostata alle nocciole

Crostata di Nocciole (Hazelnut Tart) is a traditional Italian dessert made with a rich, buttery pastry crust and a filling that typically consists of hazelnuts, sugar, and sometimes chocolate or cream.

Ingredients

For the shortcrust pastry

  • 2 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • 100ml seed oil
  • 300g white flour
  • 8g baking powder

For the filling

  • 150g hazelnuts
  • 100g sugar
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 medium eggs

For decoration

  • 50g of hazelnuts

To decorate

  • chopped hazelnuts

Steps

  1. Prepare the shortcrust pastry: mix eggs, sugar, and oil in a large bowl.
  2. When the mixture is homogeneous, add flour and baking powder and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Move to a work surface, knead by hand with flour.
  4. Wrap the dough in cling film and refrigerate for at least half an hour.
  5. For the filling, beat egg whites until stiff with an electric whisk.
  6. Add sugar to the egg whites and continue mixing with the whisk.
  7. Chop the toasted hazelnuts and blend with lemon peel to make a smooth filling.
  8. After resting, roll out the dough with a rolling pin.
  9. Place the dough in a 24 cm tart pan, buttered and floured.
  10. Line the pan with pastry, trimming the edges with a knife.
  11. Pour the filling into the pastry shell, leveling it with a spoon.
  12. Decorate with chopped hazelnuts and bake for 30 minutes at 170°C (fan) or 190°C (static).
  13. Remove the tart from the oven and serve.
